About Prayas Chess Academy

Building confident, creative thinkers through the game of chess.

Our Mission

Prayas Chess Academy aims to nurture discipline, focus, and strategic thinking in children and adults through systematic chess education.

Sessions combine classic board work, digital tools, and tournament preparation to help every student reach the next level.

Location & Timings

Address: K7-720, Near IGKC Hospital, Kalinganagar, Bhubaneswar

Batches: Weekday evening and weekend batches for beginners, intermediates, and advanced players.

Modes: Offline classroom coaching with limited batch size; online sessions on request.

Training Highlights

  • Step-by-step curriculum from rules to complex middlegame and endgame strategy.
  • Regular performance reports and feedback to parents and students.
  • Preparation for school, district, state, and national level tournaments.
  • Special sessions on tactics, openings, and practical game analysis.
